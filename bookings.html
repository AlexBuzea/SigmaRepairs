Qualified Technicians

If you are looking for local IT repair services in Cardiff you can come and drop your device at our premises.

Our experienced staff can fix a wide range of computer and laptop issues, but can also assist you in your home if you want us to come over to you and perhaps set up a device or your or fix an issue with one of your device. Alternatively, we can also pick up your device from your home if it’s within 20 miles from Cardiff.

  • Online Security Advice (securing your devices withing your house, VPNs etc.)
Hardware
  • Hard Drive Replacement & Upgrade
  • RAM Memory Upgrade
  • Keyboard Replacement
  • Power Jack Replacement
  • Fan Replacement (Noisy Computer)
  • Water Damage Issues
  • Overheating Issues
  • Battery Replacement
  • Computer Not Turning On
  • Printer, Keyboard, Mouse, Monitor Doesn’t Work
Software
  • Slow Computer*
  • Computer Freezes
  • Microsoft Office Problems or Set-up

  • Blue Screen with Errors or Other Error Messages
  • Pop-up Messages Coming Up on Screen
  • Problems Connecting to Internet, Wi-Fi Keeps Disconnecting, Slow Internets
  • Emails Problems

*some of the problems above can be caused by hardware issues as well, depending on the configuration of the computer it could be a mixture of software and hardware issues (e.g. insufficient memory or slow hard drive)
